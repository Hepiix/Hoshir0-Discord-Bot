Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Hoshir0, a Discord bot created by Hepii, collects, uses, and protects your data. By using Hoshir0, you agree to the practices described below.

Data Collection

Hoshir0 collects the following types of data:

  • User IDs: To store information such as your tarot card, fortune cookie, and virtual tokens.
  • Server and Channel IDs: To limit bot functionality to specific channels and servers as configured.

Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively for the functionality of Hoshir0, ensuring features like tarot readings, cookies, and slots work properly. The data is not shared with third parties or external services.

Data Access

The data is accessible only by the bot itself. No one else, including the creator, manually accesses or shares the data unless required for troubleshooting.

Data Removal

If you wish to have your data removed, you can contact Hepii directly on Discord: hepii. Your data will be permanently deleted upon request.

Security

Hoshir0 employs reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and you use the bot at your own risk.

Changes to the Privacy Policy
